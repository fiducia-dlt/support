Glossary

Overview

This document provides definitions for all the terms and metrics used in the Supply Chain Monitor and other TAG TrustNet documentation.

Supply Chain Monitor

Metric Description
Ad Effectiveness
Qualified Ad spend or impressions met the criteria for a qualified impression. The definition of a qualified impression requires the impression to be matched with all available buy side data (DSP, adserver and CV), is measurable, brand safe, non-IVT, with matched domains between the CV tool and DSP. This can be configured on request
Qualified Viewable Ad spend or impressions are both qualified (see Qualified above) AND viewable.
Ad Effectiveness - Data Accessibility and Consistency
Confirmed by CV tool Ad spend or impressions that have been matched from data from both the CV tool and DSP.
Confirmed by ad server, CV tool, and DSP Ad spend or impressions that were recorded by all of the following systems: adserver, CV tool, and DSP
Confirmed by ad server, CV tool, DSP, and Exchange (beta) Ad spend or impressions that were recorded by all of the following systems: adserver, CV tool, DSP, and exchange
Consistent timestamps Ad spend or impressions that had timestamps recorded within 1 minute for the adserver, CV tool, and DSP.
Unduplicated impressions Ad spend or impressions where the impressions were reported as unique by the DSP.
Ad Effectiveness - Know your supplier
Confirmed publisher domain Ad spend or impressions where the adserver, CV tool, and DSP all recorded the same domain.
Exchange works with publisher directly Ad spend or impressions where the exchange works with the publisher directly based on its log file records and sellers.json data, along with the publishers ads.txt or app-ads.txt file
Supply Chain is TAG verified Ad spend or impressions where all supply chain participants are TAG Verified
Ad Delivery Compliance
Ads.txt implemented Ad spend or impressions where the publisher has an ads.txt and app-ads.txt file.
Sellers.json implemented Ad spend or impressions where the exchange or SSP has a sellers.json file.
Consistency of ads.txt, sellers.json, and log-level data The seller ID from the SSP log file matches the seller ID (also called the Publisher’s Account ID) in the ads.txt or app-ads.txt file AND the seller ID in the sellers.json file
Supply Chain is TAG CAF Ad spend or impressions where all supply chain participants are TAG Verified
Supply Chain is TAG BSC Ad spend or impressions where all supply chain participants are TAG Verified
Delivery Quality
Measurable Ad spend or impressions were able to be measured for viewability as determined by the CV tool.
Viewable according to CV Ad spend or impressions are determined to be viewable according to the CV tool being used.
Non-Blocked Requests Ad spend or impressions where the requests were not blocked because they met the blocking criteria for the DoubleVerify Digital IQ campaign. (DoubleVerify only)
Allowed Ad spend or impressions met the blocking criteria for the DoubleVerify Digital IQ campaign and had no violations (blocks for inappropriate content, IVT, or App/Site/Language. (DoubleVerify only)
Monitored Ad spend or impressions where the DoubleVerify monitoring services were applied, were not blocked and had no violations from DoubleVerify. (DoubleVerify only)
Brand safe Ad spend or impressions determined to be brand safe as determined by the CV tool.
Non-IVT Ad Spend or impression is determined to be delivered to a human as determined by the CV tool.
Brand safe and non-IVT Ad spend or impressions determined to be both brand safe and non-IVT as determined by the CV tool.
Cost Efficiency
DSP and Exchange provided cost breakdown (beta) Ad spend or impressions where the DSP and Exchange have disclosed the media cost
DSP and Exchange provided cost breakdown with matching clearing price (beta) Ad spend or impressions where the DSP and Exchange have disclosed the media cost and clearing price records match
% Spend to Publisher The percent of the ad spend that went to the publisher for ad spend or impressions.

Other TAG TrustNet Terms

Metric Description
Ad Effectiveness A measurement of how successfully ads are being purchased that meet the goals of a campaign. The goals may include target audience, brand safety, fraud, and other criteria.
Ad Spend The total amount of money spent to purchase impressions programmatically, which includes both delivery cost and media spend.
Ad Spend Productivity A measurement of buying ads using the least amount of the ad spend to meet the goals of a campaign. This measure combines both ad effectiveness and cost efficiency
Cost Efficiency A measurement of how successfully ads are being purchased using the least amount of the ad spend.
Delivery Costs The amount of ad spend paid for impressions that includes everything except the payment to the publisher. Delivery cost typically includes platform fees paid to intermediaries (including ad servers, DSPs and SSPs) and data.
Live Compliance Performing compliance checks based on impression log-level data in near real-time of a supply chain to industry regulations, standards, and certifications.
Live Reconciliation Harmonizing, matching, and recording impression delivery log records across a supply chain in near real-time.
Media Spend The amount of ad spend paid for impressions that goes directly to the publisher.
Node Manager The TAG TrustNet product that customers log into to view the Supply Chain Monitor, configure their node, and manage users.
Supply Chain Monitor The TAG TrustNet dashboard for an agency or advertiser that uses a Sankey diagram to show data about the flow of inventory across programmatic participants, from the ad server to the publishers.
